## Areas of Expertise

Designing intelligent agents capable of autonomous planning, collaboration, and execution of complex tasks. Built on frameworks such as LangGraph and LangChain, these multi-agent LLM systems coordinate workflows and dynamically retrieve and reason over structured and unstructured knowledge.

### Bias & Fairness in AI

### Efficient Deep Learning
Leveraging techniques such as SparseGPT, Wanda, and LoRA to reduce computational overhead while preserving model accuracy and responsible behavior. The focus is on building models that are both performant and sustainable.

### Computer Vision

### Cloud & MLOps
Extensive experience with Azure, AWS, Docker, and CI/CD pipelines, ensuring that ML models not only train effectively but also scale reliably and perform robustly in production environments.
